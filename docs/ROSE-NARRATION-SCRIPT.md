# Rose narration script — Profit Pulse demo

**Tone:** Confident executive product demo. Speak to a contractor owner or CFO peer — not a screen reader.  
**Pace:** ~12–18 seconds per scene. Pause briefly between scenes.  
**Data:** All numbers and accounts are **sample demo data**.

---

## Scene 1 — Command view (`opening.mp3`)

> This is ProfitPulse — your financial command center.  
> Revenue, cash, margin, and risk on one screen, built for operators who run real jobs — not spreadsheets.

---

## Scene 2 — Profit leakage (`problem.mp3`)

> Here’s the problem most owners feel but can’t see: profit leaks quietly.  
> Labor overruns, unbilled change orders, overhead drift — margins slip when the numbers live in different places.

---

## Scene 3 — Live insight (`insight.mp3`)

> ProfitPulse pulls it together. Revenue, direct costs, net margin, and risk exposure — highlighted the moment they move.  
> You see what changed, not just what printed last month.

---

## Scene 4 — Team workflow (`workflow.mp3`)

> Your team works from the same view. Owners, ops, and finance review jobs, accounts, and projects in one workflow —  
> so “who owns this margin issue?” is never a mystery.

---

## Scene 5 — Smart alert (`alert.mp3`)

> When something’s off, ProfitPulse surfaces it early — like a copilot for your P&L.  
> “Pause spend on this job — margin is eroding.” Actionable, specific, before profit is gone.

---

## Scene 6 — Protected profit (`outcome.mp3`)

> The outcome is clarity and speed. Better decisions, faster course corrections, and margin you can defend —  
> because you’re leading from one source of truth.

---

## Scene 7 — Rose closing (`closing.mp3`)

> ProfitPulse is ready for your Rose demo.  
> Sample data only — no login, no live integrations — just the story: know your numbers, protect your margin, lead with confidence.

---

## Optional single-track fallback (`narration.mp3`)

If you record one continuous take instead of per-scene files, use the paragraphs above in order with ~1 second of silence between scenes. The player will fall back to `narration.mp3` when per-scene files are absent.
